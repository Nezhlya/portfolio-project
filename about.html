About me

I was born on 16 October in a small town, Krumovgrad, in Bulgaria. I graduated from high school in my hometown and moved to Sofia, the capital, to continue my studies. I chose to continue my studies in chemistry because of its essential value in my daily lifestyle. I got my university degree and worked for three years in a lab based in a gold mining company. I build good work discipline and healthy living habits while working there. I am currently living in the Netherlands, and after researching future career choices, I decided to continue with coding. It provides rapid employment and various career opportunities for easy career growth. The demand in the sector is soo big, and it is easy to get an education without going to university. One of the best ways to get into the coding world is to join the SheCodes platform. It teaches women worldwide with easy-to-follow educational videos, online support and affordable pricing. You can check out some of the latest coding projects in my portfolio. They are in HTML, CSS and Javascript. Some of them include platforms like Bootstrap, Font Awesome and MapTiler. In conclusion, my background and skill sets have prepared me to be a successful contributing team member in this environment. Thank you so much for your time and consideration.
